Crossing The Chasm

The most dangerous point in the lifecycle. The transition from Early Adopters (Visionaries) to the Early Majority (Pragmatists).

‚ö°

The Disconnect

Visionaries buy because the technology is new. Pragmatists buy when the technology is proven. Pragmatists don't trust Visionaries as references.

Result: You run out of money waiting for the mainstream market to activate.

The "Whole Product" Solution

To win over Pragmatists, you must offer a 100% complete solution, not just a core technology.

1. Core Product (The tech)
2. Expected Product (Support, installation)
3. Augmented Product (Integrations, standards)
4. Potential Product (Growth path)

Why the transition is so difficult.

Feature Early Adopters (Visionaries) Early Majority (Pragmatists)
Motivation Gain competitive advantage Gain productivity improvement
Risk Appetite Willing to take high risks Manage risks carefully
